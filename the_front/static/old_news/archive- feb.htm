February 14- March 7, 2009


Room 1
'tit REX: Tiny Victories

Catch the inaugural parade and ball of New Orleans' first micro-krewe: 'tit REX. Parade starts at Bacchanal Wine and Spirits 600 Poland Ave. at 5:30pm. Parade arrives for their Tiny Victories Ball at The Front at 6:30pm

Room 2
Way down in New Orleans

Curated by Aubrey Edwards, this traveling exhibition of over 30 artists, presses, zines, and collaboratives inspired by or working in New Orleans has been shown at Civilian Art Projects in DC, Super Alright Creative Studios in Austin, TX, and is now ending at The Front. Sales from this show benefit New Orleans Kid Camera Project.


Room 3
Jack Rivas: Paintings & Drawings

Nicaraguan born artist from Brooklyn, New York exhibits a series of drawings and paintings using collage, drawings, graphic elements and text inspired by leisure, domesticity, feminine attire and the many layers of glamour.

Room 4
Larry Krone: Stranger in My Mirror

This exhibition will feature artwork created during Mr. Krone's residency at Hot Iron Press.   The opening reception will include a performance at 8:00pm entitled HELP!, starring Larry Krone with special guests Jim Andralis and Renella Rose Champagne. Bring your sweetheart, this event is not to be missed!
