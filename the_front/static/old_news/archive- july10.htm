Room 1
SPORTS

SPORTS is an interactive multimedia experience by Dave Greber, Adam Montegut, Roel Miranda, Peter Leonard, and Phil Rached. 

Chicanes or "strategically placed objects", are used to slow traffic entering an area so that individuals can be monitored and evaluated. In SPORTS, we impede the progress of the gallery-goer through periodic reconfigurations of our chicanes in order to subvert typical gallery-opening pedestrian-patterns.

Room 2: Morgana King
ACNO Annex

This is not an art show. The second room of The Front will be open for non-profit business, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays 10-5, and for scheduled special events throughout the month. One of the missions of the Arts Council of New Orleans Public Art Department is community outreach and education. This "exhibit" is a foray into bridging the gap between administrators, artists, and the community. It is a space to share ideas, resources, and examples to advance public art in New Orleans and to assist artists in preparing materials for submissions.

Rooms 3 & 4
TILL MORNING

Till Morning is a group exhibition featuring the work of Rachel DeTrinis, Kelly Gazlay, and Erika Somogyi.  These three artists are all makers of things, using handmade principles of construction, painting, and newer media.  The issue of time arises in the works, through the visibly repetitious processes used by the artists in the act of creation, and a space embedded in Americana traditions and the History of Pop Culture.  The exhibition concentrates on relationships that become fixed, while the world changes and moves forth, the two, or group of individuals, remain permanently engaged and rooted in time.  Peter and Wendy, and the brood of children, all try exhaustibly to protest this unreliable world, an irregular state of being, in which one cannot predict or control any outcome.
