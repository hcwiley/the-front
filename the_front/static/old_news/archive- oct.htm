Room 1 & 2
Jonathan Traviesa
Portraits

This collection of portraits by New Orleans photographer Jonathan Traviesa delivers compelling insights into the city and its inhabitants. Each portrait captures the New Orleans characters in a back yard, on the front porch, or in another intimate setting, with the collection as a whole painting a vivid portait of the city itself and the many ways people choose to live in it.
