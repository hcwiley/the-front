Rooms 1 - 3: Worn Out
Recent work by Midwest Pressed (Tim Dooley and Aaron Wilson)

Midwest Pressed is stuffing The Front floor to ceiling with fresh, hot screen printed goodness. Worn-Out is a barrage of cliché tempered with an optimistic enthusiasm for printed matter. A gallery wall will be filled with retinal spendor, screenprinted right before your eyes on Saturday March 10th during the opening and Friday March 16th, until they are thrown out. Check out this live-printing event and meet the people responsible for Midwest Pressed.
